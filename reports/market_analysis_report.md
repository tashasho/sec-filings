### Key Fund Market Observations

- **Hedge Fund Peak**: Hedge fund filings peaked in 2021-2022 amid volatile markets and alternative investment demand, before declining with rising interest rates.

- **VC Fund Surge**: Venture capital fund filings tripled from 2015 to 2021, driven by the startup boom, unicorn minting, and retail participation via platforms like AngelList and Republic.
